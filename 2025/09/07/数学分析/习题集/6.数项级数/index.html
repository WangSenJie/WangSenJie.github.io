жӯЈйЎ№зә§ж•°

гҖҗеҚҺеӣӣ/жқҺгҖ‘и®Ёи®әд»ҘдёӢзә§ж•°зҡ„ж•ӣж•ЈжҖ§

  1. $\sum\limits_{n=3}^{\infty}\frac1{n(\ln n)^p(\ln n)^q}$.
    жіЁпјҡ $\sum\limits_{n=3}^{\infty}\frac1{n(\ln n)^p(\ln n)^q}\begin{cases}ж”¶ж•ӣ,& p>1 \ жҲ– \ (p=1~\text{дё”}~q>1),\\ еҸ‘ж•Ј, &p<1 \ жҲ– \ (p=1 ~\text{дё”}~ q\le 1).\end{cases}$
  2. $\sum\frac{1}{x^{\ln n}} \ (x>0)$.
    жіЁпјҡ дәӢе®һдёҠ $\frac{1}{e^{\ln n}}=\frac{1}{n^{\ln 3}}$
  3. $\sum\frac{n!}{(x+1)(x+2)\cdots(x+n)}\quad(x>0)$.
    жіЁпјҡ еҲ©з”Ё Raabe еҲӨеҲ«жі•
  4. $\sum\left[\frac{(2n-1)!!}{(2n)!!}\right]^p, \ (p\in\mathbb{N}^+)$.
    жіЁпјҡ RaabeеҲӨеҲ«жі•
  5. $\sum\left[\frac{(2n-1)!!}{(2n)!!}\right]^{\frac{1}{n}}$.
    жіЁпјҡ и®° $u_n=\frac{(2n-1)!!}{(2n)!!}$, еҲҷжңү $\lim\limits_{n\to\infty}\frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n}=1$, дәҺжҳҜеҲ©з”ЁдёҚзӯүејҸ $\underline{\lim}\limits_{n\to\infty}\frac{u_{n+1}}{u_n}\le\underline{\lim}\limits_{n\to\infty}\sqrt[n]{u_n}\le\overline{\lim}\limits_{n\to\infty}\sqrt[n]{u_n}\le\overline{\lim}\limits_{n\to\infty}\frac{u_{n+1}}{u_n}$ еҚіеҫ—еҺҹзә§ж•°йҖҡйЎ№и¶ӢдәҺ $1\neq0$, д»ҺиҖҢеҸ‘ж•Ј.
  6. $\sum\left[\frac{(2n)!!}{(2n+1)!!}\right]^p$.
    жіЁпјҡ еҲ©з”Ё Wails е…¬ејҸ: $\lim\limits_{n\to\infty}\left[\frac{(2n)!!}{(2n-1)!!}\right]^2\frac{1}{2n+1}=\frac{\pi}{2}$.
  7. $\sum\frac{a_n}{(1+a_1)(1+a_2)\cdots(1+a_n)} \ (a_n>0)$.
    жіЁпјҡ жіЁж„ҸеҲ° $\frac{a_n}{(1+a_1)(1+a_2)\cdots(1+a_n)}=\frac{1}{(1+a_1)(1+a_2)\cdots(1+a_{n-1})}-\frac{1}{(1+a_1)(1+a_2)\cdots(1+a_n)}$.
  8. $\sum\limits_{n=1}^{\infty}\left[1-\left(\frac{n-1}{n+1}\right)^{k}\right]^{p} \ (p,k>0)$.
    жіЁпјҡ жіЁж„ҸеҲ° $\left(\frac{n-1}{n+1}\right)^{k}=\left(1-\frac{1}{n}\right)^{k}\left(1+\frac{1}{n}\right)^{k}=1-\frac{2k}{n}+o(\frac{1}{n})$.
  9. $\sum\limits_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{n^{n-2}}{e^nn!}$.
    жіЁпјҡ еҸҜд»Ҙи®Ўз®— $\frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n}$. д№ҹеҸҜд»ҘеҲ©з”Ё Strling е…¬ејҸ $n!\sim \sqrt{2n\pi}\cdot \left(\frac{n}{e}\right)^n$.
  10. $\sum\limits_{n=3}^{\infty}\frac{1}{\ln n\ln\ln n}$.
    жіЁпјҡ з§ҜеҲҶеҲӨеҲ«жі•.
  11. $\sum\limits_{n=3}^{\infty}\frac{1}{(\ln n)^{\ln\ln n}}$.
    жіЁпјҡ иҖғиҷ‘ $\lim\limits_{n\to\infty}\frac{\ln\frac{1}{a_n}}{\ln n}$.
  12. $\sum\limits_{n=3}^{\infty}\frac{1}{(\ln\ln n)^{\ln n}}$.
    жіЁпјҡ иҖғиҷ‘ $\lim\limits_{n\to\infty}\frac{\ln\frac{1}{a_n}}{\ln n}$.

гҖҗеҚҺеӣӣ12.2дҫӢ6гҖ‘и®Ёи®әзә§ж•° $1+b+bc+b^2c+b^2c^2+\cdots+b^nc^{n-1}+b^nc^n+\cdots$ зҡ„ж•ӣж•ЈжҖ§пјҢе…¶дёӯ $0<b<c$.
жіЁпјҡ жіЁж„ҸеҲ° $\underline{\lim}\limits_{n\to\infty}\frac{u_{n+1}}{u_n}=b,\overline{\lim}\limits_{n\to\infty}\frac{u_{n+1}}{u_n}=c$.

гҖҗеҚҺеӣӣ12.2дҫӢ8гҖ‘иҖғеҜҹзә§ж•° $b+c+b^2+c^2+\cdots+b^n+c^n+\cdots$ зҡ„ж•ӣж•ЈжҖ§пјҢе…¶дёӯ 0<b<c<1.
жіЁпјҡ жіЁж„ҸеҲ° $\underline{\lim}\limits_{n\to\infty}\sqrt[n]{u_n}=\sqrt{b},\overline{\lim}\limits_{n\to\infty}\sqrt[n]{u_n}=\sqrt{c}$.

гҖҗеҸІе®ҡзҗҶ14.3.5гҖ‘и®ҫ $\lbrace u_{n}\rbrace$ жҳҜд»»ж„Ҹзҡ„жӯЈж•°еҲ—, йӮЈд№Ҳ
$$
\underline{\lim}\limits_{n\to\infty}\frac{u_{n+1}}{u_n}\le\underline{\lim}\limits_{n\to\infty}\sqrt[n]{u_n}\le\overline{\lim}\limits_{n\to\infty}\sqrt[n]{u_n}\le\overline{\lim}\limits_{n\to\infty}\frac{u_{n+1}}{u_n}.
$$
жіЁпјҡ еҸӘйңҖиҜҒжҳҺеҸіеҚҠйғЁеҲҶ, и®° $\overline{\lim}\limits_{n\to\infty}\frac{u_{n+1}}{u_n}=q$ ж №жҚ®дёҠжһҒйҷҗзҡ„е®ҡд№ү, еҪ“ $n$ е……еҲҶеӨ§ $n\ge N$ ж—¶жңү $\frac{u_{n+1}}{u_n}<q+\varepsilon$, дәҺжҳҜдёҚйҡҫеҫ—еҲ° $u_n<u_N(q+\varepsilon)^{n-N}\Rightarrow \sqrt[n]{u_n}<\sqrt[n]{u_N(q+\varepsilon)^{-N}}\cdot(q+\varepsilon)\Rightarrow \overline{\lim}\limits_{n\to\infty}\sqrt[n]{u_n}\le q+\varepsilon\to q \ (\varepsilon\to0)$.

гҖҗеҚҺеӣӣ12.2е®ҡзҗҶ12.9гҖ‘ (з§ҜеҲҶеҲӨеҲ«жі•) и®ҫ $f$ дёә $[1,+\infty)$ дёҠйқһиҙҹеҮҸеҮҪж•°пјҢйӮЈд№ҲжӯЈйЎ№зә§ж•° $\sum f(n)$ дёҺеҸҚеёёз§ҜеҲҶ $\int_1^{+\infty}f(x)\mathrm{~d}x$ еҗҢж—¶ж”¶ж•ӣжҲ–еҗҢж—¶еҸ‘ж•Ј.
жіЁпјҡ жіЁж„ҸеҲ° $f_n\le\int_{n-1}^nf(x)\mathrm{~d}x\le f(n-1)$.
